Lasagna Recipe:

Recipe Description:

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish.

Lasagna Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Meat Sauce
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs

Lasagna preparation Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. In a large pot, bring water to a boil
  3. Add salt to the boiling water
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente
  5. Drain the noodles and set them aside
  6. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  7. Prepare the Cheese Mixture
  8. Assemble the Lasagna
  9. Bake
  10. Rest and Serve
